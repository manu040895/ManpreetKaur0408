About Me

Manpreet is consistent, patient and constantly growing.

Manpreet got her undergraduate Engineering degree in Electronics and Telecommunication. Upon graduation, Manpreet joined IBM as a Software Engineer, and worked on Java, Python, SQL, Web Development, Adobe Experience Manager, Adobe Analytics, IBM Cloud, WebSphere and Bash Automation for the Global Business Services (GBS) team. She was appreciated for her innovation initiatives and quality of work.


Inspired by the potential of data analytics while working on Adobe Analytics, she joined the Data Analytics masters program at the Northeastern University. She firmly believes in using analytics as part of a process and not as an end in itself; for Manpreet, setting clear business questions and using technology in the right place to reach the end solution is the way to go.


Her masters degree projects/courses are focused mainly around machine learning and data engineering while leveraging business, quantitative and visualization tools to do just that. Manpreet interned in an Investment Management Company - GMO with am aim to learn the Finance World while exhibiting her data engineering capabilities at work.


Meanwhile, she voluntarily joined a Ventilator startup featured in Forbes India -Ultimate 120 innovations in COVID and took charge of its software development and operation plans of expanding sales across globe. She is mentored by Northeastern University for key making business decisions.

In the future, Manpreet focuses to work on resolving business challenges using her experience in different roles and contribute to the ever evolving world
